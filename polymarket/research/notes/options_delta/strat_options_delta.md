# Strat — Options-Delta (OD)


## Summary

- Scope: Strat — Options-Delta (OD) in the OD/options-delta area.
- Existing takeaway/status: Options-delta is the valuation/signal layer carved out of Block K. Standalone OD is closed as a taker/valuation system, while source-clean rich-short passive Strategy A remains a small-capital live-measurement candidate inside the MM lifecycle.
- Evidence lives in the detailed sections below; this summary is only a navigation layer over the existing note.

## One-line thesis

`btc-updown-4h-*` literally **is** a cash-or-nothing digital struck at the window-open price. Polymarket **systematically overprices the longshot side and short-dated vol** (K6 +24 vol pts far/late; K7 cross-category longshot premium). OD's edge is to **sell the overpriced side and carry to resolution**, harvested through passive maker fills (the MM lifecycle), with **delta-aware risk caps**. The external Binance delta hedge is a *minor* variance overlay — Strategy A v2 Phase 2 proved it cuts variance only ~10% in the far-|z| gold mine (delta is tiny there), so it is NOT where OD earns its keep.

## Scope (what belongs here)

OD = the **valuation/signal layer**. In: digital-option fair value, implied vs forecast vol, the longshot/vol-overpricing harvest (sell the rich side), moneyness/time regime selection, fair-value entry filtering, **dollar-delta inventory caps**, source-basis filtering, and (as a minor overlay) an external delta hedge. Out (→ [[strat_market_making]]): the *execution* layer — passive quoting mechanics, queue, adverse-selection measurement, pure spread+rebate capture. The two share the K5/K-PEG maker lifecycle as common infra; OD decides *what/when/how-much*, MM decides *how to get filled*.

> **Why OD ≠ "MM with a hedge":** the maker lifecycle (passive fills, two-sided, carry) is MM execution.
> OD's distinct content is the *pricing bias* it exploits (longshot/vol overpricing → a directional lean
> AGAINST the rich side, risk-capped) plus the *valuation signals* that gate entry and sizing. Strip those
> out and you're left with neutral spread capture = MM. The hedge is the least important OD lever.
