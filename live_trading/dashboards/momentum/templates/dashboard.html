Entry conditions

▸ How entry works
  • E1 EMA — Close > EMA (uptrend filter)
  • E2 Volume — Volume > Vol_MA (confirms participation). Not used for BTC/AVAX.
  • E3 Caution gate — Caution_Long blocks entry. ADX override: if ADX > adx_override, caution is bypassed.
  • E4 Validity — Swing highs and ATRs must be non-NaN.

Caution flags

▸ How caution works
  • C1 OBV divergence — Price at highs but OBV below OBV_MA. Unsupported move.
  • C2 Overextension — (Swing_Hi_Cau − Low) > 1.5 × ATR_Cau. Range stretched too wide.
  • Either flag sets Caution_Long = True, blocking new entries unless ADX override is active.
